Present Perfect or Simple Past

What is the difference?

Simple Past

1. My dog died!

This is a finished action

2. I went to New York in Nov,2012.

With specific times or dates.

Present Perfect

1. I've lived in Brazil for many years.

This is an unfinished action.

2. I have been to New York.

This is a life experience.

3. Joe has painted the house.

Recently completed action.
